Know your tweeters!

Beware of fraud tweeteratis who use Black marketing sites to gain followers. Buying fake followers is as easy as ordering a snack on Swiggy. They can spread fake news and incite voilence therefore it is important for you to know your Tweeteraties. Individual Confidence Index

This represents the individual user's Confidence Index, by appling the neural network model on the given user. This is index should not be considered when the given user is a influcener/celebrity/leader or a person with high twitter activity. For such users use Cummulative Confidence Index.

Cummulative Confidence Index

This will fetch 20 most recent followers of the account and fetch their data and apply the neural network model on each follower and give the avg score. This index sholud be considered when the given user is a influcener/celebrity/leader or a person with high twitter activity.
